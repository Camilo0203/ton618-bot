## Long description

```text
TON618 is a bot-first operational platform for Discord communities that need structured support workflows.

It combines ticket management, SLA tracking, live playbooks, incident mode, audit history, and staff productivity tools in one Discord-native workflow.

TON618 launches with a simple commercial model:
- Free for core ticketing
- Pro for advanced operations like SLA rules, auto-assignment, incident mode, daily reports, and live playbooks
- Supporter status for donations and recognition only

The product is English-first, bot-first, and designed for communities with active support or moderation teams that want less chaos and more operational control.
```

## FAQ

**What makes TON618 different?**

TON618 is built for staff operations, not generic community features. It focuses on tickets, SLA, playbooks, incident handling, and operational visibility.

**How does pricing work?**

V1 uses a Free + Pro + Supporter model. The bot owner activates plans manually in Discord. Donations only enable supporter recognition.

**Do I need the dashboard?**

No. TON618 is bot-first. The dashboard is optional.

**Who is it for?**

Communities with active staff teams: gaming, SaaS support, creator communities, and projects that need more structure than a basic ticket bot.

**What language does it launch in?**

English-first. Spanish remains as partial fallback where already supported.
